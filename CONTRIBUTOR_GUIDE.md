## How to Become a Contributor

### Eligibility Requirements

To apply for verified contributor status, you must:

- [x] Have an active LocaleLore account for at least 30 days
- [x] Have submitted at least 10 approved facts
- [x] Maintain a quality score of 4.0 or higher
- [x] Have no content guideline violations
- [x] Agree to contributor terms and conditions

### Application Process

1. **Build Your Portfolio**:
   - Submit high-quality facts
   - Get them approved
   - Earn positive ratings (likes, comments)
   - Maintain good standing

2. **Apply**:
   - Navigate to Profile ‚Üí Contributor Program

   - Application reviewed within 5-7 business days
   - We assess:
     - Content quality
     - Consistency

     - Expertise
     - Community standing

4. **Approval**:
   - ‚úÖ **Approved**: You'll receive a notification and contributor badge
   - ‚ùå **Rejected**: You can reapply after 60 days (we'll provide feedback)

### After Approval

## Content Guidelines

### What Makes Great Content?

**High-Quality Facts Include:**

‚úÖ **Accuracy**: Verified, factual information  
‚úÖ **Depth**: Detailed explanations (200+ words ideal)  
‚úÖ **Engagement**: Interesting, storytelling approach  
‚úÖ **Originality**: Unique insights, not copy-pasted from Wikipedia  
‚úÖ **Visuals**: High-quality, relevant images  
‚úÖ **Sources**: Citations for historical/factual claims (when applicable)  

### Content Categories

Focus on your areas of expertise:

- **History**: Historical events, figures, buildings
- **Culture**: Traditions, festivals, local customs
- **Food**: Local cuisine, restaurants, recipes
- **Nature**: Parks, landmarks, geographical features
- **Architecture**: Buildings, monuments, urban design
- **Art**: Public art, museums, cultural institutions
- **Hidden Gems**: Local secrets, off-the-beaten-path spots
- **Events**: Festivals, markets, seasonal happenings

### Content Standards

**Required:**
- Minimum 100 words (200+ recommended)
- At least one high-quality image
- Proper grammar and spelling
- Accurate location pin
- Appropriate category tag

**Prohibited:**
- ‚ùå Plagiarized content
- ‚ùå Misleading or false information
- ‚ùå Promotional/advertising content
- ‚ùå Offensive or inappropriate material
- ‚ùå Personal opinions without context
- ‚ùå Copyright violations

### Image Guidelines

**Image Requirements:**
- High resolution (minimum 1200px wide)
- Well-lit and in focus
- Relevant to the fact
- Properly oriented
- Max file size: 5MB

**Image Rights:**
- Own the rights or have permission
- Provide attribution if required
- No watermarks from other sources
- Creative Commons OK (with attribution)

**Photo Tips:**
- Shoot during golden hour (soft lighting)
- Include people for scale and interest
- Capture unique angles
- Show details and context
- Edit for color and clarity

## Quality Score System

### How Quality is Measured

Your quality score (1-5 stars) is calculated from:

**Content Quality (40%):**
- Writing quality (grammar, style, clarity)
- Depth and detail
- Accuracy and research
- Originality

**User Engagement (30%):**
- Likes and reactions
- Comments and discussions
- Shares and saves
- View time

**Community Feedback (20%):**
- Peer ratings
- Admin assessments
- Flag reports (negative impact)

**Consistency (10%):**
- Regular submissions
- Maintaining standards
- Improvement over time

### Quality Score Tiers

- **5.0 Stars**: Exceptional (Top 5%)
- **4.5-4.9**: Excellent (Top 20%)
- **4.0-4.4**: High Quality (Top 50%)
- **3.5-3.9**: Good (Average)
- **3.0-3.4**: Acceptable (Minimum)
- **< 3.0**: Below Standard (Warning)

### Improving Your Score

**Tips to Boost Quality:**

1. **Research Thoroughly**: Don't just rely on surface-level info
2. **Tell Stories**: Make facts engaging and narrative
3. **Add Context**: Explain why something matters
4. **Use Great Photos**: Invest in quality imagery
5. **Cite Sources**: Show your work is credible
6. **Engage with Comments**: Respond to questions
7. **Update Outdated Facts**: Keep content current
8. **Learn from Feedback**: Apply suggestions from admins

### Quality Score Consequences

**High Score (4.0+):**
- Maintain contributor status
- Eligible for bonuses
- Priority review
- Considered for Expert tier

**Medium Score (3.0-3.9):**
- Standard processing
- No bonuses
- Encouragement to improve

**Low Score (< 3.0):**
- Warning notification
- 30-day improvement period
- Risk of contributor status removal
- Additional review required

## Best Practices

### Content Creation Tips

1. **Research First**:
   - Read multiple sources
   - Visit the location if possible
   - Interview locals
   - Verify facts

2. **Write Engagingly**:
   - Start with a hook
   - Use storytelling techniques
   - Break into readable paragraphs
   - Add interesting details
   - End with a call-to-action

3. **Optimize for Discovery**:
   - Use descriptive titles
   - Add relevant tags
   - Choose accurate location
   - Select appropriate category
   - Write good meta descriptions

4. **Engage with Community**:
   - Respond to comments
   - Thank users for likes
   - Answer questions
   - Incorporate feedback

5. **Stay Active**:
   - Submit regularly (aim for 2-5 facts/week)
   - Maintain consistent quality
   - Update old facts when needed
   - Participate in challenges

### Time Management

**Efficient Workflow:**

1. **Batch Research** (1 hour):
   - Research 5-10 topics at once
   - Save sources and notes
   - Take photos

2. **Write** (30 minutes per fact):
   - Draft content
   - Edit for clarity
   - Add formatting

3. **Edit & Polish** (15 minutes):
   - Check grammar
   - Verify facts
   - Optimize images

4. **Submit & Monitor** (5 minutes):
   - Submit fact
   - Track approval
   - Engage with feedback

**Total Time**: ~1 hour per fact (with practice, you'll get faster)

### Maximizing Earnings

**Strategic Approach:**

1. **Focus on Popular Categories**:
   - Food and restaurants
   - Hidden gems
   - Historical landmarks
   - Photo-worthy locations

2. **Target High-Traffic Areas**:
   - Major cities
   - Tourist destinations
   - Trending locations
   - Underserved areas (opportunity!)

3. **Publish Timely Content**:
   - Seasonal events
   - Upcoming festivals
   - New openings
   - Current trends

4. **Build a Series**:
   - "Hidden Gems of [City]"
   - "Food Tour: [Neighborhood]"
   - "Historical Walking Tour"
   - Create related content

5. **Promote Your Facts**:
   - Share on social media
   - Engage with comments
   - Update with new info
   - Cross-reference related facts
