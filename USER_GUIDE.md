## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit LocaleLore**: Navigate to [your-domain].lovable.app
2. **Click "Sign Up"**: Find the button in the top right corner
3. **Choose Sign-Up Method**:
   - Email & Password
   - Google Sign-In
4. **Complete Your Profile**:
   - Add a display name
   - Upload a profile picture (optional)
   - Add a short bio (optional)

### First Steps

After creating your account:

1. **Explore the Map**: See facts from around the world
2. **Search for Locations**: Find content near you or places you're interested in
3. **Follow the Tutorial**: Complete the onboarding tour (if available)
4. **Submit Your First Fact**: Share something interesting about your area

## Contributing Facts

### How to Submit a Fact

1. **Click "Add Fact"** (+ button on map or in navigation)
2. **Choose Location**:
   - Click on the map
   - Search for an address
   - Use your current location
3. **Add Details**:
   - **Title**: Short, descriptive headline (required)
   - **Description**: Tell the story (required, min 50 characters)
   - **Category**: Select the most appropriate category
   - **Images**: Upload photos (optional, max 5MB each)
   - **Tags**: Add relevant keywords (optional)
4. **Review & Submit**: Check your fact and click "Submit"

### Content Guidelines

‚úÖ **Good Facts:**
- Accurate and verifiable information
- Interesting local knowledge
- Historical context
- Cultural insights
- Hidden gems and local tips

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- Inaccurate or false information
- Personal opinions without context
- Spam or promotional content
- Offensive or inappropriate material
- Copyright violations

### Fact Status

After submission, your fact goes through moderation:

- **Pending**: Waiting for review
- **Approved**: Live on the map
- **Rejected**: Did not meet guidelines (you'll receive feedback)

**Approval Time**: Usually within 24-48 hours

### Editing Your Facts

1. Go to your **Profile** ‚Üí **My Facts**
2. Click on the fact you want to edit
3. Make changes
4. Submit for re-review

**Note**: Approved facts require re-approval after editing.

## Gamification & Rewards

### How It Works

Earn **XP (Experience Points)** by contributing to LocaleLore:

- Submit a fact: **50 XP**
- Fact gets approved: **100 XP bonus**
- Fact gets liked: **5 XP per like**
- Fact gets commented: **10 XP per comment**
- Daily login: **10 XP**

### Levels

As you earn XP, you'll level up and unlock rewards:

- **Level 1-5**: Beginner (0-500 XP)
- **Level 6-10**: Explorer (501-2,000 XP)
- **Level 11-20**: Adventurer (2,001-10,000 XP)
- **Level 21-50**: Expert (10,001-50,000 XP)
- **Level 51+**: Legend (50,000+ XP)

**Level Benefits:**
- Unlock new badges
- Access to rewards shop
- Leaderboard recognition
- Special profile customization

### Achievements

Unlock achievements by completing specific challenges:

- **First Steps**: Submit your first fact
- **Social Butterfly**: Make 10 friends
- **Explorer**: Submit facts in 10 different cities
- **Popular**: Get 100 likes on your facts
- **Streak Master**: Log in 30 days in a row

**View Your Achievements**: Profile ‚Üí Achievements

### Rewards Shop

Spend your **available points** on rewards:

- **Badges**: Display special badges on your profile
- **Custom Titles**: Add unique titles ("Legend", "Explorer", etc.)
- **Avatar Borders**: Customize your profile picture border
- **Feature Unlocks**: Early access to new features

**Access Rewards Shop**: Gamification ‚Üí Rewards

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "I can't log in"
- Check your email and password
- Try resetting your password
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

#### "Map isn't loading"
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Allow location permissions
- Try disabling browser extensions

#### "My fact isn't showing up"
- Wait for approval (24-48 hours)
- Check your "My Facts" page for status
- Review content guidelines
- Contact support if rejected without reason

#### "Images won't upload"
- File size must be < 5MB
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
- Try compressing the image
- Check internet connection

#### "Notifications not working"
- Check notification settings
- Allow browser notifications
- Refresh the page
- Log out and back in

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is LocaleLore free to use?**
A: Yes! LocaleLore is completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future, but core functionality will always be free.

**Q: Can I use LocaleLore without an account?**
A: You can browse the map and view facts, but you need an account to submit facts, earn XP, and use social features.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Not yet, but our website works great on mobile browsers! A native app is coming in the future.

**Q: How do I become a verified contributor?**
A: See our [Contributor Guide](CONTRIBUTOR_GUIDE.md) for details.

### Content Questions

**Q: How long does fact approval take?**
A: Usually 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification when reviewed.

**Q: Can I edit my facts after submission?**
A: Yes! But edited facts require re-approval.

**Q: What if someone steals my content?**
A: Report it using the flag icon. We take copyright seriously.

**Q: How do I delete a fact I submitted?**
A: Profile ‚Üí My Facts ‚Üí [Select fact] ‚Üí Delete

### Gamification Questions

**Q: What's the difference between XP and Points?**
A: XP determines your level. Available Points are earned from XP and can be spent in the Rewards Shop.

**Q: Do points expire?**
A: No, your points never expire.

**Q: Can I see a leaderboard?**
A: Yes! Visit Gamification ‚Üí Leaderboard

### Privacy Questions

**Q: Who can see my profile?**
A: By default, everyone. You can change this in Privacy Settings.

**Q: Can I make my facts anonymous?**
A: Not currently, but this feature is planned.

**Q: How do I download my data?**
A: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Download My Data

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone.
